About LandVista Properties Limited

Our Story

Founded in the heart of Nairobi, LandVista Properties Limited was born from a simple vision: to make land ownership accessible, transparent and rewarding for every Kenyan.

With years of experience in the Kenyan real estate market, we understand the unique challenges and opportunities that come with land investment in our beautiful country.

From the bustling outskirts of Nairobi to the serene landscapes of Kithyoko, Juja-farm and Kivandini, we’ve helped thousands of families and investors find their perfect plot.

Our commitment goes beyond just selling land. We believe in building lasting relationships, providing expert guidance and ensuring every transaction is handled with the utmost professionalism and care.
